Project Name - Valentia,The Living Dream

Location - Vengurla , Maharashtra

Status - Proposed

Built-up area - 55200 sq.ft.

Description - The contoured site surrounded by the grand landscape of mountains on one side and beaches on the other, provides an opportunity to design spaces within the immediate context. The project provides luxury living to the people habitating the place within a mixed use development proposal. The scope of the project is to provide residential, commercial and hotel layout within the plot.
