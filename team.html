About Our Team

We are a team of young, dynamic and experienced Architects with relevant experience in the field of designing and execution.

The team has handled varied portfolios from Residential, Commercial, Institutional and Hospitality. These include various low-rise townships, independent bungalows, commercial spaces and various interior design projects. Our Team is further distinguished with their specialist planners, engineering experts and qualified contractors for desirable results.

Founder & Principal Architect

Ar. Saurabh Barde

M. Arch Environmental Architecture-UK

Ar. Saurabh Barde is a Practising Architect and Founder of EN Architecture and Designs having more than 12 years of Experience in the field of Architecture, Landscape and Sustainable Building Designs. He has keen interests in practising a wide variety of projects of different scales. He expertises in the field of Architecture Design, Town planning, Landscape Design, Sustainable building Design and Research in Architecture.

He has the ability to lead the Architecture team with passion and energy thus completing various projects with desired results. His designs are socially and economically viable. He envisions all his projects with a vision of sustainability and competence.

His interest in architecture goes beyond the practice wherein he‘s been teaching students at various architectural institutions in Mumbai (Kamla Raheja college of Architecture and Rizvi college of Architecture). Along with that, he‘s been involved in writing various architectural papers for PLEA (Passive Low Energy and Architecture) , Publishing Books and Articles.
