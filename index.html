About EN+AD

EN Architecture and Designs is a collaborative association of Architects and Designers that focuses on integrating intellect into the design. We are a versatile architectural firm which provides all sort of architectural solutions for wide range of projects of various scales.

We, at EN+AD not only focus on user sensitive designs but also integrates Comprehensive construction technology in projects to get satisfactory end results.

Our Design Philosophy

At EN+AD, we see each project as a new opportunity to unlearn, learn and explore various architectural solutions which makes the project unique.

Aesthetics, Innovation and Sustainability are the key components of our designs. However, we make sure that our spaces do not compromise on its functionality and user-responsiveness.
